Introduction to Enkryption

Encryption is the process of using an algorithm to transform information to make it unreadable for unauthorized users.

The science of encrypting and decrypting information is called cryptography. In computing, unencrypted data is also known as plaintext, and encrypted data is called ciphertext.

The formulas used to encode and decode messages are called encryption algorithms or ciphers.
